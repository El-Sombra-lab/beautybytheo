About Beautybytheo

Welcome to Beauty by Theo, where beauty meets expertise, and every brushstroke tells a unique story. At Beauty by Theo, we understand that makeup is more than just a cosmetic application – it's an art form that empowers individuals to express themselves and embrace their inner beauty. Founded by Theo, a passionate and skilled freelance makeup artist, our mission is to provide personalized, transformative beauty experiences that leave our clients feeling confident, empowered, and, above all, beautiful.








Our Mission

At Beauty by Theo, our mission is clear – we aim to enhance the natural beauty of every individual we touch. We believe that beauty is diverse and unique, and our approach to makeup is rooted in the idea that each person is a canvas waiting to be adorned. Through personalized consultations and expert application techniques, we strive to create looks that not only complement your features but also reflect your personality and style. We are committed to fostering a sense of confidence and self-expression in every client, ensuring that their beauty journey with us is nothing short of extraordinary.








Our Vision

Our vision at Beauty by Theo is to be a beacon of creativity and inspiration in the beauty industry. We aspire to redefine beauty standards by celebrating individuality and embracing the diversity of beauty in all its forms. We envision a world where makeup is a tool for self-love and empowerment, where every stroke of the brush tells a story of confidence, strength, and authenticity. As we continue to grow, our goal is to be a trusted partner on your beauty journey, offering not just makeup services, but a transformative experience that leaves a lasting impact on how you perceive and celebrate your own unique beauty. Join us on this journey of self-discovery and beauty exploration at Beauty by Theo.
